Beef Stroganoff

Indulge in the creamy goodness of this classic Beef Stroganoff recipe. Tender ground beef, sautéed mushrooms, and scallions are smothered in a luscious sauce made with dijon mustard, cream cheese, and savory seasonings. The dish is served over a bed of buttery egg noodles that perfectly complements the rich flavors of the stroganoff. Each bite is a heavenly combination of meaty and velvety textures, with a satisfying umami taste that will make your taste buds dance. This recipe is perfect for a cozy family dinner or a fancy gathering, guaranteed to leave your guests wanting more. Don't miss out on this mouthwatering meal, give it a try today!

Ingredients

  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 5 White Mushrooms
  • 6 Scallions
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1/4 oz Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 Pound Egg Noodles
  • 1 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 2 tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Prep

    Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil. Peel and finely chop 1 teaspoon garlic. Trim and discard ends from mushrooms, then thinly slice. Trim and discard ends from scallions, then thinly slice.

  2. Cook Veggies

    Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a medium skillet over medium-high. Add chopped garlic, mushrooms, ¾ of the scallions, and a pinch each salt and pepper; cook, stirring, until mushrooms are tender and browned, 3–5 minutes.

  3. Cook Beef

    Add ground beef and a pinch of salt to same skillet; cook, stirring and breaking beef up into smaller pieces, until browned, about 5 minutes. Stir in 1 tablespoon flour and cook until toasted, 1 minute. Add Dijon and 1¼ cups water; bring to a boil over high, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until thickened, about 3 minutes.

  4. Cook Noodles

    Meanwhile, add egg noodles to boiling water and cook, stirring, until tender, 5–6 minutes. Drain noodles, then return to pot, and stir in 2 tablespoons butter off the heat; season to taste with salt and pepper.

  5. Finish

    To same skillet, add cream cheese and stir until melted, about 1 minute; season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve egg noodles topped with beef stroganoff and garnish with remaining scallions. Enjoy!
